Split Marble Collection

Split Marble Collection is inspired by the sawing process of the marble. Being cut artificially, the retaining raw rims of stone pieces greatly contrast with the smooth profile. Through juxtaposing, the common way to place marble slabs, the rough edges create the image of the a layered cavern beneath the table and the stool. The stiff outlines together with the irregular fractures present the aesthetic of dissonance.
