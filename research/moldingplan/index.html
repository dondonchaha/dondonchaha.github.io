Research of Molding Plan

Ningxia Road in Taipei City, Taiwan, where the wood industry and resources gather, has been a significant place of woodworking since Japanese-occupied period. The street is with profound traditional manufacturing base and techniques. Yet, due to offshoring, the industry in Ningxia Road is no longer flourishing as before.

In order to fully understand the industrial condition of Ningxia Road, several visits were paid. In lumber shops, huge accumulated stocks of moldings are kept. The molding, the ready-made finishing material, used to play an essential role in home decor in early days while it is being forgotten nowadays. As a result of a wide research on Ningxia Road, the molding thus becomes the main topic of the project Molding Plan, hoping to propose a new way to rethink this architectural material.

The whole series of Molding Plan are manufactured by wood craftsmen in Ningxia Road, utilizing plentiful resources of an age-old place in Taipei City as well as bringing people’s attentions to the traditional woodworking industry.
