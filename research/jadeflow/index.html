Research of Jade Flow

The initial idea of the project was to invite creatures of nature to act as co-designers, which is at the intersection of art and life science.

Phototaxis is a kind of taxis that occurs when a whole organism moves towards or away from stimulus of light. After conducting abundant researches on behavioral biology, the project focused on the phototaxis and aimed to merge such significant taxis into a lighting design. The phototaxic algae was chosen as the main theme.
