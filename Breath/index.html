Breathe

Breathe is a living lighting installation that rotates in response to human behavior. Walking by Breathe, light varies. The inspiration comes from gentle movements of light and shadow under the tree.

When a person walks by and pauses, pendants will start to rotate, causing the installation to move. Conversely, the pendants return to a motionless state once the sensor detects active movement by the viewer, giving way to a poetic morphing of space and people.

By creating a neat trick of light, Breathe explores the dynamic relationship between people and the surroundings.
